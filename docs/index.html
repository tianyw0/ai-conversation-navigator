Navigate Your ChatGPT Conversations with Ease!

ChatGPT Conversation Navigator is a user script that enhances your ChatGPT experience by adding a clickable navigation sidebar. Easily jump to any part of your long conversations.

Key Features

  • Adds clickable conversation indexes to the ChatGPT page.
  • Automatically tracks and highlights your current position.
  • Supports both light and dark themes.
  • Updates navigation automatically with new messages.
  • Clean design and intuitive functionality.
